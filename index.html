Dynamic Noise Reduction using Deep Learning

A solution for Noiseless Meetings

Introduction

Noise pollution is a major issue affecting our health and well-being. Machine learning techniques can be used to reduce noise pollution and improve our quality of life.

How it Works


Step 1

Collect noise data from different sources such as microphones, sensors, and cameras.

Step 2

Analyze the data using machine learning algorithms to identify noise patterns and sources.

Step 3

Use noise reduction techniques such as filtering, sound masking, and noise cancellation to reduce noise pollution.
